Preferred Networks operates the multi-tenant Kubernetes-based PFCP cloud for AI/ML workloads. This talk covers isolation (RBAC enforcement, host/process/data separation, network isolation), fair resource control, and billing on a shared cluster, plus accelerator support for AI/ML and RDMA/HPC, and how the system scales across sites.
